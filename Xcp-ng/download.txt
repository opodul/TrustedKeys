Grab the latest 8.2 ISO here, (https://mirrors.xcp-ng.org/isos/8.2/xcp-ng-8.2.1.iso?https=1)
then create your bootable USB key with:

dd if=xcp-ng-8.2.1.iso of=/dev/sdX bs=8M oflag=direct
Then just boot on it!

On Windows, you can use Rufus to create the bootable USB stick.
(https://rufus.akeo.ie/)
